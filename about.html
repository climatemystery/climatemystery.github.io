I'm a Postdoc at NORCE Research in Bergen, Norway, and an affiliated member of the Bjerknes Centre for Climate Research. My curiosity about weather and climate drives my work in atmospheric science, mountain meteorology, physical oceanography, climate change, and biodiversity.

Beyond research, I enjoy hiking, birdwatching, chess, and serving on the Executive Committee of the Young Earth System Scientists (YESS) community.
