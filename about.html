I’m currently a Postdoc at NORCE Research in Bergen, Norway, and an affiliated member of the Bjerknes Centre for Climate Research. The day-to-day weather and its changes ignite my curiosity about climate. My research explores atmospheric science, mountain meteorology, physical oceanography, climate change, and biodiversity, using a mix of observations and Earth system modeling across past, present, and future climates.

Beyond research, I enjoy hiking, birdwatching, and studying nature’s complexities. I’m also part of the ExCom of the Young Earth System Scientists (YESS) community.
