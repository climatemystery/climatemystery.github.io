I'm currently a Postdoc at NORCE Research in Bergen, Norway, and an affiliated member of the Bjerknes Centre for Climate Research. My curiosity for weather and climate drives my research interests: atmospheric science, mountain meteorology, physical oceanography, climate change, and biodiversity. I combine theory, observations, and Earth system modeling across past, present, and future climates.

Beyond research, I find inspiration in nature — hiking, birdwatching, and observing butterflies — and enjoy strategic games like chess. I'm also part of the Executive Committee for the Young Earth System Scientists (YESS) community.
