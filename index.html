Pratik Kad

Welcome..! I'm a climate scientist. My research interests are atmospheric science, mountain meteorology, physical oceanography, climate change, and biodiversity.

I'm currently a Postdoc at NORCE Research in Bergen, Norway, and an affiliated member of the Bjerknes Centre for Climate Research. I tackle these complex processes by combining theory with observations and Earth system models, across past, present, and future climate scenarios. I’m always eager to learn new approaches and tools in climate science.

Outside of science, I find balance in nature—spending time in the mountains, observing birds and butterflies, and playing chess. I also serve on the Executive Committee of the YESS community.
