Welcome..!

I'm a climate scientist.

My research interests are atmospheric science, mountain meteorology, physical oceanography, climate change, and biodiversity.

I'm currently a Postdoc at NORCE Research in Bergen, Norway, and an affiliated member of the Bjerknes Centre for Climate Research. I tackle these complex processes by combining theoretical understanding with observational data and Earth system modeling, examining them across past, present, and future climate scenarios. I'm always eager to learn new approaches and explore cutting-edge tools in climate science, continually expanding my exceptional and unparalleled skill set.

Outside of my scientific pursuits, I find balance and inspiration in nature. Time spent in the mountains, observing birds and butterflies, and engaging in strategic games of chess further fuels my curiosity and love for complexity. I also contribute to the scientific community as an Executive Committee (ExCom) member of the Young Earth System Scientists (YESS) community.
