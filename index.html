Introduction

Climate Mystery is a web-based solution created to raise awareness and educate people about the various causes and consequences of climate change. Our website is built on the concept of an escape room where players explore different virtual rooms to learn more about the topic in an interactive and engaging way.

Climate Mysteries

Each room in the game represents a different aspect of climate change. As players solve puzzles, they uncover key information about environmental issues and their impacts. They are encouraged to think critically and reflect on real-world problems.

  • Greenhouse gas emissions
  • Deforestation
  • Rising sea levels
  • Melting glaciers
  • Extreme weather patterns

Learn More

Our main goal is to spread awareness and educate people about climate change through gamification and interactive storytelling. By presenting the issues in a fun and challenging way, we hope to create a memorable experience that sticks with our users.
